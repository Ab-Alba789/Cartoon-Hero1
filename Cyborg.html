Cyborg

About Cyborg

Cyborg, AKA Victor Stone is part man, part android. He was put together by his father after an accident almost caused him to die. Now, he uses is technology to fight crime. He is a member of the Justice League.

Cyborg checks out his abilities

Characteristics

  1. Large portions of Victor Stone's body have been replaced by advanced mechanical parts (hence the name Cyborg) granting him superhuman strength, speed, stamina, and flight.

  2. His mechanically-enhanced body, much of which is metallic, is far more durable than a normal human body. Cyborg's internal computer system can interface with external computers.

  3. Cyborg is consistently depicted as tinkering over time with his cybernetic parts, enhancing his functions and abilities to levels beyond those set by his father.
