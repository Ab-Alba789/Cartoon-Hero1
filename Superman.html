Superman

About Superman

Superman is an alien from Planet Krypton who was raised on Earth. He has super powers that he uses to protect the City of Metropolis and the world.

Superman fights for his city.

Characteristics

  1. He has super Strength, Speed; laser and Xray vision, which he uses to fight crime.
  2. He's a big believer of Justice
  3. He is very optimistic, unlike Batman

Friends

Superman has many friends:

  • Batman
  • Wonder Woman
  • Aquaman
  • Green Lantern
