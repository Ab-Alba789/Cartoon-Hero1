Aquaman

About Aquaman

Aquaman is king of Atlantis. He is a Superhero who has the telepathic ability to communicate with marine life, which he can summon from great distances. He is one of the founding members of the Justice League.

Aquaman looks on from his Throne

Characteristics

  1. He can breathe underwater and possesses a superhuman physique strong enough to withstand attacks from superhuman opponents and resist machine gun fire

  2. He can swim at extremely high speeds, capable of reaching speeds of 3,000 meters per second

  3. He can see in near total darkness and has enhanced hearing, granting limited sonar.

Friends

Aquaman has many friends in the Justice League:

  • Superman
  • Batman
  • Wonder Woman
  • Green Lantern
