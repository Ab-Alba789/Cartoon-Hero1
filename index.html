Batman

About The Bat

The Batman is a vigilante from Gotham City who fights crime and strikes fear in the hearts of his enemies Batman is the alias of Bruce Wayne, a wealthy American playboy, philanthropist, and industrialist. He seeks vengeance against criminals after witnessing the murder of his parents Thomas and Martha Wayne; he trains himself physically and intellectually, crafts a bat-inspired persona, and monitors the Gotham streets at night. He is one of the founding members of the Justice League.

Batman fights for his city.

Characteristics

  1. Batman has no inherent superhuman powers; he relies on "his own scientific knowledge, detective skills, and athletic prowess". Batman's inexhaustible wealth gives him access to advanced technologies, and as a proficient scientist, he is able to use and modify these technologies to his advantage

  2. Batman is regarded as one of the world's greatest detectives, if not the world's greatest crime solver. Batman has been repeatedly described as having a genius-level intellect, being one of the greatest martial artists in the DC Universe, and having peak human physical conditioning. As a polymath, his knowledge and expertise in countless disciplines is nearly unparalleled by any other character in the DC Universe. He has traveled the world acquiring the skills needed to aid him in his endeavors as Batman. Superman considers Batman to be one of the most brilliant minds on the planet.

  3. Batman utilizes a vast arsenal of specialized, high-tech vehicles and gadgets in his war against crime, the designs of which usually share a bat motif. Batman's body armored costume incorporates the imagery of a bat in order to frighten criminals
